Golubtsi

  • Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Serves: ???
Ingredients Quantity
cabbage rice
rice 110g
beef 400g
carrot 2

Step 1: Cook rice in plenty water the same way you would pasta until al dente for approximately 5 minutes after the water boils. It shouldn't be too soft. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Grate carrots. In a large bowl combine beef, pork, parsley, onions, carrots, rice, salt and pepper.

Step 3: Place your head of cabbage on a cutting board, cut off the bottom and pull the leaves away from the stalk. Repeat with the second head of cabbage.

Step 4: Preheat your oven to 350F/180C.

Step 5: Pour the remaining sauce over cabbage rolls, cover with a lid and bake in the oven for 1 hour. Then take the lid off and bake for 30 more minutes. Serve with additional sour cream or creme fraiche and fresh herbs like dill and parsley.
